🎮 About the Game

Barsh Guess It! is a fun and educational picture-guessing game designed for toddlers and young children. Players look at a close-up image and try to guess what it is from multiple-choice answers — building vocabulary and visual recognition skills.

Game worlds include:

🐄 Farm Animals 🍎 Fruits 🚗 Vehicles 🧸 Toys 🦁 Safari 🍭 Sweets 🎵 Music 🌊 Ocean 🌸 Flowers 🐾 Pets + 10 more worlds

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app free?

Yes, Barsh Guess It! is completely free to download and play. It is supported by non-personalized advertisements.

Are there in-app purchases?

No. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or any real-money transactions. Additional worlds can be unlocked by completing previous worlds or by watching an optional free rewarded video ad.

Is this app safe for my child?

Yes. The app is designed for ages 3–5 and is fully COPPA compliant. All ads are non-personalized and rated G (General Audiences). No personal data is collected from children. Game progress is stored only on your device.

What languages does the app support?

The app supports both English and Hebrew. You can switch languages from the character selection screen.

How do I delete my child's data?

All data is stored locally on your device. To delete it, simply uninstall the app. There is no data stored on external servers.

Why are there ads?

The app is free, and we use child-safe, non-personalized ads from Google AdMob to support continued development. Ads are limited in frequency — they only appear after completing 4–6 levels, after finishing a world, or if you choose to watch one to unlock content.

My child lost their progress — what happened?

Progress is saved automatically on your device. If progress was lost, it may be due to uninstalling/reinstalling the app or clearing app data. Unfortunately, we cannot restore progress as it is not stored on servers. Contact us and we'll do our best to assist.

The app is crashing — what should I do?

Please try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, try reinstalling. If it still doesn't work, please contact us at barsbuildme@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version.
