2. App Description

Barsh Guess It! is a free educational guessing game for children ages 3–5. The App features:

  • 20 themed worlds (animals, fruits, vehicles, toys, safari, sweets, and more)
  • Picture-guessing gameplay with multiple-choice answers
  • 6 playable characters: Pipo, Lulu, Bobo, Barsh, Tiki, and Milo
  • A sticker album that fills as levels are completed
  • Support for English and Hebrew
