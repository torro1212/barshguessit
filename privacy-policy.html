Privacy Policy

Barsh Guess It! — Children's Educational Game

Ages 3–5 · COPPA Compliant

Last updated: March 30, 2026 · Effective immediately

Welcome to Barsh Guess It! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you or your child uses our app. This app is designed for children ages 3–5 and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU GDPR, and Apple's App Store privacy requirements.
⚠️ Important Notice for Parents & Guardians This app is directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All advertising displayed is non-personalized and child-directed. Please read this policy to understand our practices.

1. About This App

  • App Name: Barsh Guess It!
  • Developer: Shahar Sussman
  • Contact: barsbuildme@gmail.com
  • Target Audience: Children ages 3–5
  • Platform: iOS (App Store)
  • Content: Educational picture-guessing game with 20 themed worlds (animals, fruits, vehicles, toys, and more)
  • Languages: English and Hebrew

2. Information We Collect COPPA

A. Game Progress Data (Stored Locally on Device)

  • Player nickname chosen by the parent/child
  • Selected game character (Pipo, Lulu, Bobo, Barsh, Tiki, or Milo)
  • Language preference (English or Hebrew)
  • Completed levels, collected stickers, unlocked worlds, and trophies
  • Current level and world progress

All game data is stored exclusively on the device using Apple's AsyncStorage. We do not transmit, upload, or back up this data to any server we control.

B. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

  • Device type and operating system version (collected automatically by AdMob)
  • App version
  • Ad interaction data (impressions and clicks) — processed entirely by Google
  • IDFA / Advertising ID: Per COPPA, the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) dialog is intentionally suppressed for all users. IDFA is not collected or used. All ads are served as non-personalized, child-directed.

C. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Real name, email address, phone number, or physical address
  • Photos, videos, or microphone recordings
  • Location data
  • Social network accounts or contacts
  • Payment or financial information (no in-app purchases)

3. How We Use Information

  • Game Features: Locally stored progress enables the child to continue from where they left off, collect stickers, unlock worlds, and earn trophies.
  • Advertising: Google AdMob displays child-safe, non-personalized banner, interstitial, and rewarded video ads to support the free app.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymous usage data is processed by AdMob to help us understand which parts of the app are most used. This data is not linked to any individual child.

4. App Tracking Transparency & COPPA COPPA

This app targets children under 13. In full compliance with COPPA, we have configured Google AdMob with the following flags:

  • tagForChildDirectedTreatment = true — Enables COPPA mode in AdMob
  • tagForUnderAgeOfConsent = true — Enables child-safe mode for GDPR
  • requestNonPersonalizedAdsOnly = true — All ad requests are non-personalized
  • maxAdContentRating = G — Only family-safe (General) ad content

The ATT permission dialog ("Allow tracking") is intentionally not shown. The Advertising Identifier (IDFA) is never accessed or collected.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party SDK:

  • Google AdMob — serves banner, interstitial, and rewarded video ads.
    All requests are sent with child-directed treatment enabled.
    Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
    Google's COPPA Policy: support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

We do not use analytics SDKs (Firebase, Amplitude, Mixpanel, etc.), social SDKs, or any other third-party data processors beyond AdMob.

6. Advertising in the App

The app is free and supported by ads. Ads appear in the following contexts:

  • Banner Ad: A small banner displayed at the bottom of the Album screen.
  • Interstitial Ad: A full-screen ad shown after completing every 4–6 game levels (randomized), and after completing a full world.
  • Rewarded Ad: An optional ad the user can choose to watch in order to unlock a locked world. The user must explicitly tap a button to initiate this — it is never forced.

Ads are never shown back-to-back. A rewarded ad is never immediately followed by an interstitial. All ad content is limited to General (G) rating suitable for children.

7. In-App Purchases & Virtual Currency

This app contains no in-app purchases and no real-money transactions of any kind. All content is free or unlockable through gameplay or by watching an optional rewarded ad. No payment information is ever collected.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

All game data is stored locally on the device and is never sent to our servers. To delete all data associated with this app:

  • Delete all data: Uninstall the app from your device. This permanently removes all locally stored game progress.
  • Reset progress within the app: Not currently available (data can only be cleared by uninstalling).

Since we do not operate user accounts or cloud storage, there is no account deletion flow — there is nothing stored on our servers to delete.

9. Parental Rights COPPA

As a parent or guardian of a child using this app, you have the right to:

  • Review what data is stored (all data is local, on-device only)
  • Delete your child's data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us at barsbuildme@gmail.com with any concerns

If you believe your child has inadvertently provided personal information, please contact us immediately and we will investigate and resolve the issue promptly.

10. Security

  • Game data is stored using React Native AsyncStorage (device-level storage).
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers.
  • All AdMob network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • We do not store, process, or have access to any data on external servers.

11. International Users

This app may be used globally. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, or similar jurisdictions, please note:

  • We apply tagForUnderAgeOfConsent = true to AdMob for all users, consistent with GDPR Article 8 (child consent requirements).
  • No personal data of EU/UK residents is transferred outside those regions by us. AdMob's data processing is governed by Google's GDPR data processing terms.
